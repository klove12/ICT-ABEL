Privacy Policy

Welcome to ICT Abel!

At ICT Abel, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website and engage with our services. We’re committed to keeping your data safe and transparent about how it’s handled.

1. What Information We Collect

When you use our website and services (live trade, mentorship, and education), we collect the following information to provide a seamless and personalized experience:

  • Personal Details: When you sign up for our services, we gather your name, email address, phone number, and any additional details you choose to share with us.
  • Payment Information: For secure transactions, we collect payment information, which is processed through trusted third-party systems.
  • Site Usage: To better understand how our users engage with the website, we collect data such as pages visited, the time spent on each page, and interactions with our content.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us to:

  • Deliver Our Services: Whether you’re engaging in live trading, accessing educational materials, or receiving mentorship, we use your data to ensure a top-notch experience.
  • Stay in Touch: We’ll occasionally send you updates, valuable insights, and news about our services and offers. You can opt out anytime!
  • Enhance Your Experience: We use this information to analyze site performance and make improvements, making sure your interaction with us is always top-tier.

3. Sharing Your Information

We take privacy seriously and only share your data in the following cases:

  • Trusted Partners: We work with trusted third-party companies, such as payment processors, to ensure your experience is smooth and secure.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, we may share your information with authorities to comply with legal obligations.

We’ll never sell your data or share it without your consent.

4. How We Keep Your Data Safe

Your security is important to us! We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption, firewalls, and secure servers, to protect your data. We continually monitor and improve our security practices to keep your information safe from unauthorized access.

5. Your Rights and Choices

We believe you should have full control over your information:

  • You can access, update, or delete your personal data anytime.
  • If you’d like to stop receiving our communications, simply unsubscribe via email, and we’ll respect your choice.

6. Cookies & Tracking

To improve your experience, we use cookies. These small files help us analyze how you use our website and deliver personalized content. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. However, please note that some website features may not function properly without them.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to keep it in line with new laws or changes in how we operate. Any major updates will be posted here, and we’ll notify you as necessary. By continuing to use our services, you agree to these updates.
